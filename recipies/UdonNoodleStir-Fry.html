Udon Noodle Stir-Fry

Description

Enjoy a delightful and satisfying meal with this Udon Noodle Stir-Fry recipe. Udon noodles are the star of this dish, paired with a medley of fresh vegetables and a flavorful sauce. This recipe is quick, versatile, and perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of udon noodles
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms (shiitake or cremini)
  • 2 cups of chopped leafy greens (bok choy, spinach, or kale)
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the udon noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and sliced onion to the pan. Saut√© until fragrant and the onion becomes translucent.
  4. Add the julienned carrot, sliced bell pepper, and mushrooms to the pan. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), sesame oil, rice vinegar, and sugar to make the sauce.
  6. Pour the sauce mixture into the pan with the vegetables and stir to coat evenly.
  7. Add the cooked udon noodles and chopped leafy greens to the pan. Toss everything together until well combined and the noodles are heated through.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning and sauce quantities according to your preference.
  9. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, stirring continuously, until the flavors meld together.
  10. Remove from heat and garnish with sliced green onions, sesame seeds, and cilantro, if desired.
  11. Serve the Udon Noodle Stir-Fry hot and enjoy!

Feel free to experiment with additional ingredients like tofu, shrimp, or chicken to add protein and customize the stir-fry to your liking. This versatile recipe allows you to explore different vegetable combinations and adjust the flavors according to your taste. Enjoy the wonderful textures and flavors of this Udon Noodle Stir-Fry!
