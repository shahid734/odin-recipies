Ramen Reciepe

Description

Indulge in the comforting and savory flavours of homemade ramen noodles width easy to follow recipe. Ramen is a popular Japanese known for its delicious broth and tender noodles. Get ready to savor a bowl of steaming hot ramen that will warm your soul.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of ramen noodles
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of miso paste
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 2 cups of sliced vegetables (bok choy, mushrooms, carrots, etc.)
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs
  • Thinly sliced green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken or vegetable broth and water to a boil.
  2. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, soy sauce, and sesame oil to the pot. Stir well to combine the flavors.
  3. Reduce the heat to a simmer and let the broth cook for about 15-20 minutes to infuse the flavors.
  4. While the broth is simmering, cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a separate pan, sauté the sliced vegetables in a little oil until they are tender but still retain some crunch.
  6. Once the broth is ready, strain it to remove the garlic and ginger. Return the strained broth to the pot.
  7. Dissolve the miso paste in a small bowl with a ladleful of hot broth. Stir until the miso paste is well incorporated.
  8. Add the miso mixture to the pot and stir gently to combine.
  9. Divide the cooked ramen noodles among serving bowls.
  10. Pour the hot broth over the noodles, making sure to cover them completely.
  11. Top the ramen with the sautéed vegetables, soft-boiled eggs, and sliced green onions.
  12. Customize your ramen with additional toppings like sliced cooked pork, seaweed, sesame seeds, or chili oil.
  13. Serve the ramen noodles immediately while hot and enjoy the comforting flavors.

Feel free to adjust the seasonings and toppings according to your preferences. With this homemade ramen recipe, you can create a satisfying and flavorful meal that rivals your favorite ramen shops. Get ready to slurp and savor the deliciousness of homemade ramen!
